Our Story

A Salinas Valley gathering place

Tucked on Front Street in Soledad, The Windmill Restaurant has become the local go-to for hearty breakfasts, hometown supper-club classics, and a full bar poured with a little mixologia flair. Farmworkers, families, and travelers off Highway 101 share the same booths — and the same generous portions.

Regulars keep coming back for the steak marsala, the all-day breakfast menu, and service that consistently goes above and beyond. It's the kind of clean, comfortable dining room where conversation carries easily and nobody rushes you out the door.
