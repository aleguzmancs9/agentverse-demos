Where the Valley Sits Down to Eat

Named for the windmills that once turned across the Salinas Valley farmland, The Windmill Restaurant has served Soledad as the town's all-day gathering spot — part diner, part supper club, part neighborhood bar. Guests come for chicken fried steak and huevos rancheros at breakfast, linger over skillets and burritos at lunch, and settle in for steak marsala, seafood, and hand-crafted mixologia cocktails once the sun goes down.

Locals and travelers off Highway 101 alike keep coming back for the same reasons reviewers mention again and again: generous portions, a kitchen that treats every plate seriously, service that goes above and beyond, and a dining room comfortable enough for a long, easy conversation.
