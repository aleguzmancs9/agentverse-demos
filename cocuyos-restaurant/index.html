A homey table on Kidder Street

Made from scratch, served with heart.

Cocuyos is a casual, family-run kitchen where the tortillas are pressed by hand and the salsa is made fresh daily. Neighbors come for the generous portions and hardworking, friendly staff — and they stay for the kind of authentic flavor that feels like a trip home.

Grab a seat on our outdoor patio, order a michelada or fresh horchata, and let the kitchen do what it does best.
