OUR STORY

A homey table, lit like a summer night

In Spanish, cocuyos means fireflies — the little glowing lights that appear over Soledad's fields on warm evenings. It's that same warmth we bring to every plate: casual, unhurried, and full of heart.

Cocuyos is a family-run, casual eatery on Kidder Street serving traditional-style Mexican dishes made the way abuela taught — hand-pressed tortillas, slow-simmered sauces, and salsas made fresh in the molcajete. Pull up a chair on our outdoor patio and settle in.

Guests keep coming back for the generous portions, the fast and friendly service, and prices that feel like a gift. It's the kind of place where everyone — vegans, kids, and abuelitos alike — finds something to love.
